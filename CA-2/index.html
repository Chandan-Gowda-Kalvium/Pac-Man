The-Pac-Man



!! Instructions To Play !!

1. The player controls Pac-Man, who must eat all the dots inside an enclosed maze while avoiding four colored ghosts.
2. Eating each pac-dots gives you 1 point
3. Power palets are found in four corners of the map.
4. Each Power palet gives you 10 points.
5. Touching the power palets turns the ghosts into defencive mode !!
6. You have to kill 2 ghosts for the game to end (not more or less "2 ghosts and 2 ghosts only".
7. Once your game is over, your score is displayed !!
